Summary of Version 1

Reason/source

Version 1 demonstrates the user journey for an intensive farmer.

The intensive farming sector has specific user needs that differ from all other sectors and has the most sites and users of all sectors.

We hypothesised it would be simpler for the user if, rather than asking the user to determine their site's emissions using the formula in the guidance, intensive farmers could count livestock and how they are housed.

The service could then do the calculations to determine site emissions.
